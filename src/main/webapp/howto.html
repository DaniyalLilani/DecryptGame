How to Play Decrypt

Ready to test your decryption skills against others? Here's how to dive into Decrypt.

Getting Started

Decrypt is a multiplayer game that requires at least 2 players. The game is played across a remote server, connecting you with others ready to challenge your cipher-solving abilities.

Gameplay

  • A game starts only when there are at least 2 players in the server.
  • Once a game begins, a timer is set for 1 minute.
  • Your objective is to decipher the given cipher within the time limit.
  • Enter your solution into the remote chat server to submit your answer.
  • The first person to submit the correct decryption earns a point.
  • The game continues until a player scores 5 points.

Winning

Put your speed and wits to the test! Be the quickest to decrypt and reach 5 points before your competitors to win the game.
